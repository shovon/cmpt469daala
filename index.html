What Is Daala?

A video codec created to either matches or exceeds the quality of current-generation of video codecs, such a VP9 and HEVC. Currently an ongoing effort by Xiph and Mozilla to introduce a royalty-free video codec.

Let's Look At JPEG

What Does JPEG Do?

  • Spacial to frequency conversion
    • similar to audio but for images, and instead of 1D, we use 2D
  • Fourier's Theorem
    • “A wave is the sum of many sinusoidal waves.”
  • DFT is often used to extract the sine waves of a wave (FFT for the fast variant)
  • But we're going to use the DCT
    • Benefit over DFT
      • no complex numbers involved
      • wave coefficient packed closer to lower frequencies
